Slices are half-open by design: `stop` is excluded, lengths are easy to compute, and adjacent ranges line up without off-by-one glue.

```python
record = "SKU-0012:BR:active"
sku = slice(0, 8)
country = slice(9, 11)

record[sku], record[country]  # ('SKU-0012', 'BR')
```
